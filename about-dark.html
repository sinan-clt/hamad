Our Story

Founded with a vision to redefine ceramic excellence, Hamed Ceramics has grown from a modest workshop to a leading name in premium ceramic tiles. Our journey began with a simple belief - that beautiful, durable surfaces should be accessible to everyone.

Over the years, we've combined traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology to create ceramic solutions that stand the test of time. Each tile we produce reflects our commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainable manufacturing practices.

"We don't just make tiles - we craft surfaces that transform spaces and inspire living."

Our Products

Wall Tiles

Our premium wall tile collection offers endless design possibilities with superior durability. From classic subway tiles to contemporary geometric patterns, we have solutions for every aesthetic.

Floor Tiles

Engineered for both beauty and performance, our floor tiles withstand heavy foot traffic while maintaining their elegance. Available in various finishes including matte, polished, and textured.

Custom Solutions

Our design team works closely with architects and homeowners to create bespoke ceramic solutions for unique projects. Let us bring your vision to life with custom sizes, colors, and patterns.

Our Manufacturing

State-of-the-Art Facility

Our 50,000 sq. ft. manufacturing plant houses the latest ceramic production technology from Italy and Germany. Automated quality control systems ensure every tile meets our exacting standards before it leaves our facility.

Sustainable Production

We've invested in water recycling systems and energy-efficient kilns that reduce our environmental impact. 85% of our production waste is recycled back into the manufacturing process.
